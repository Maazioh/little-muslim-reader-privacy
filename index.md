# Privacy Policy for Little Muslim Reader

**Effective Date:** 04/28/2026
**Last Updated:** 04/28/2026

Little Muslim Reader ("the App," "we," "our," or "us") is an iOS application that provides illustrated Islamic stories for children. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what we do — and importantly, what we do not do — with your information.

## The Short Version

**Little Muslim Reader does not collect any personal information.** Everything you do in the app — your name, favorite stories, reading progress, and streak — is stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to us or to any third party.

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to:

- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Your age, location, or device identifiers
- Your reading habits, favorites, or progress
- Any analytics, crash reports, or usage data
- Any data through advertising networks (we do not show ads)
- Any data through third-party SDKs (we do not use any)

## Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device using Apple's standard `UserDefaults` system:

- A name you optionally enter during onboarding
- The list of stories you have marked as favorites
- Your current page in each story you have started
- The list of stories you have completed
- Your reading streak (consecutive days you have read)
- The date you first opened the app

This information **never leaves your device.** It is not backed up to our servers because we have no servers. If you delete the app, all of this information is permanently deleted.

You can clear all of this stored data at any time by going to **Profile → Settings → Reset Progress** within the App.

## Children's Privacy

Little Muslim Reader is designed for children and complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple's Kids Category requirements. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user — including children — there is no information about children for us to access, share, or sell.

## Third-Party Services

Little Muslim Reader does not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising networks, or SDKs.

## Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection to function. All story content and illustrations are bundled with the App at the time of download.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through an update to the App.
